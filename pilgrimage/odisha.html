Experience the Rich Heritage and Culture of Odisha on our Puri - Konark - Bhubaneswar Tour!

Upcoming available dates:Jun 30,2023,Jul 28,2023 Are you looking to explore the rich heritage and culture of Odisha? Look no further than Haiku Holidays' Puri - Konark - Bhubaneswar tour package. Our package offers a 2-night and 3-day trip to Puri, Konark, and Bhubaneswar that includes round-trip economy air tickets, 2-night deluxe accommodation in Bhubaneswar, authentic local food, an experienced guide, and all applicable taxes. The package costs only Rs.24900 per person.

We take care of everything, from your comfortable and hassle-free journey to your spiritual quest. Our package includes visiting some of the most iconic and spiritually significant places in Puri, Konark, and Bhubaneswar.



Day1 - In Bhubaneswar, you will visit :

Lingaraj Temple: One of the most iconic temples in Bhubaneswar, dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is known for its carvings, stunning architecture, and peaceful ambiance. A visit to Lingaraj Temple is a must for anyone visiting Bhubaneswar.

Parsurameswar Temple: A temple devoted to Lord Shiva, famous for its beautiful carvings and stunning design.

Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves: A must-visit destination for history lovers, located on the outskirts of Bhubaneswar. The caves are carved into two adjacent hills and are known for their historical significance,Complex carvings, and stunning architecture.

Day 2, you will visit the beautifu Konark Sun Temple and the surrounding areas:

Konark Sun Temple: A: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most famous landmarks in India. The temple is dedicated to Lord Surya and is known for its stunning architecture and carvings.

Ramchandi Temple: A temple located on the banks of the Bay of Bengal and known for its beautiful surroundings and peaceful ambiance.

Chandrabhaga Beach: A beach located near the Sun Temple. The beach is known for its beautiful scenery and peaceful ambiance. Take a dip in the water or simply relax on the beach and soak in the sun.

Lord Jagannath Temple: A temple located in Puri and one of the most significant pilgrimage sites in India. It is dedicated to Lord Jagannath and is known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful ambiance.

Golden Beach: A beautiful beach that is perfect for relaxation and enjoying the beautiful surroundings.

On Day 3, you will visit the City of Cuttack and explore the following places:

Dhableshwar Temple: A temple dedicated to Lord Shiva and known for its stunning architecture and serene surroundings.

Cuttack Chandi Temple: A temple dedicated to Goddess Chandi and known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful ambiance.

Maritime Museum: A museum that showcases the maritime history of India and is a must-visit destination for history buffs and those interested in naval history.
