Day 1: Arrival in Kasi
Your Kasi Flight Trip will begin with a flight from Chennai to Varanasi. Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our representative who will transfer you to your hotel. After freshening up, you will head out to explore the city's most famous temples - Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Vishalakshi Temple, and Annapoorni Temple. In the evening, witness the captivating Ganga Aarthi and soak in the divine aura of the city. Overnight stay at Kasi.

Day 2: Spiritual Exploration in Kasi
The second day of the trip begins with a spiritual experience. You will start your day with tharpanam and boating on the Ganges river. Afterward, you will visit Manikernika Ghat & Harichandra Ghat, where you can witness the traditional Hindu funeral rituals. Later, you will visit the Sankat Mochan Temple (Hanuman Temple), Manasa Temple, and Kala Bhairavar Temple. In the evening, you can indulge in some shopping before heading back to your hotel for an overnight stay.

On the final day of your trip, you will have some free time for shopping and exploring the city on your own. Later, you will be transferred to the airport for your return flight to Chennai. Take home unforgettable memories of your spiritual journey in Kasi with Haiku Holidays. ReadMore
