Are you looking for a romantic getaway with your partner? Haiku holidays honeymoon trip to Manali, Kullu, and Shimla is the perfect way to start your life's journey together. This 5 nights and 6 days trip offers you a perfect blend of adventure, romance, and sightseeing. Let's dive into the itinerary and explore what awaits you on this unforgettable honeymoon trip


The package includes round trip air tickets, private transfers and sightseeing, 3-4 star standard accommodation with breakfast, and all currently applicable taxes. Please note that the package excludes everything except for the inclusions mentioned above.


Day 1 : Arrival at Chandigarh - Manali

Your honeymoon trip starts as soon as you arrive at Chandigarh airport, where our chauffeur will welcome you. From there, you will be taken to Manali, which is an 8-hour drive covering a distance of 250 km from Chandigarh. For those travelling from Delhi, the drive is approximately 14 hours covering a distance of 550 km. Check-in to the hotel and get some rest.

Day 2 : Manali Sightseeing

After having breakfast at the hotel, get ready to explore the beautiful valley of Manali. You will visit Tibetan Monastery and Vashist Village, two popular tourist attractions. In the afternoon, you are free to enjoy yourself and retire for the night at the hotel.

Day 3 : Manali - Solang valley - Manali

The third day of your honeymoon trip is all about experiencing the thrill of the Solang valley. Play with snow and enjoy the scenic beauty of this popular tourist spot. You can also opt for skiing at an additional cost of Rs. 600 per person. Return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 4 : Manali - Kullu - Shimla

Check out from the hotel and proceed to Shimla, covering a distance of 250 km in approximately 7 hours. Enroute, visit Kullu and spend some time there. Check-in to the hotel in Shimla and spend the night.

Day 5 : Shimla Sightseeing

After breakfast, head to Fagu and Kufri, two of the most popular tourist spots in Shimla. In the afternoon, visit the Indian Institute of Advanced Studies, Jakhu Temple, and the Sankat Mochan Temple. In the evening, go shopping at The Mall and The Ridge before returning to the hotel for the night.

Day 6 : Chandigarh - Drop - Chennai

This is the last day of your honeymoon trip. After breakfast, our chauffeur will drive you back to Chandigarh, the same way you travelled on day 1. From there, we will drop you off at the airport. We hope you have made unforgettable memories on your honeymoon trip.

If you are a honeymoon couple looking for an unforgettable trip, Haiku holidays Manali - Kullu - Shimla Honeymoon Trip is just for you. Book your trip now and start your journey together with unforgettable memories .
