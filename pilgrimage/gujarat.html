Welcome to Gujarat, the land of diverse culture, history, and breathtaking natural beauty. This state in western India is known for its rich heritage, exquisite temples, and the famous Asiatic lions found in the Gir Forest. Embark on a 7-day adventure to explore the wonders of Gujarat with our comprehensive package that includes all your travel, accommodation, and food expenses.



With our package:

Day 1: Arrival in Rajkot and Proceeding to Dwaraka:
Your Gujarat Trip begins as you arrive in Rajkot from Chennai. From there, you will proceed to Dwaraka, where you will check-in at your 3-star accommodation. Spend the night relaxing and getting ready for an exciting day ahead.

Day 2: Exploring Dwaraka:
On day 2, you'll explore the holy city of Dwaraka. Visit the Rukmani temple, Bet Dwaraka (Boating), Nageshwar Jyothirlinga, Mul Dwaraka, and Dwarakdeesh Temple. These sacred sites will offer you a glimpse into the spiritual side of Gujarat. Overnight stay in Dwaraka.

Day 3: Porbandar and Somnath:
After breakfast, proceed to Somnath, en route visiting Porbandar, Gandhiji Birth palace, Kasthuriba Birth Place, Somnath Joythirlinga, and Triveni Mahasangam. These historical and cultural sites will give you a sense of the state's rich past. Spend the night at Gir

Day 4: Jungle Safari at Gir and Bhavnagar
Start your day with an early morning Jungle Safari at Gir Forest, which is known for its wildlife sanctuary and lion sanctuary. After that, proceed to Bhavnagar and check-in at your 3-star accommodation. Spend the night relaxing and getting ready for the next day

Day 5: Nishalank Mahadev Temple and Shopping
After breakfast, visit the Nishalank Mahadev Temple and spend some time shopping (if time permits). This will give you a chance to buy some souvenirs to take back home. Spend the night in Ahmedabad.

Day 6: Sardar Vallabhai Patel Statue and Swami Narayan Temple
After breakfast, proceed to Vadodara and visit the Sardar Vallabhai Patel Statue, the world's most giant statue. After that, visit the Swami Narayan Temple and check in at your 3-star accommodation. Spend the night relaxing and getting ready for the next day.

Day 7: Local Sightseeing in Ahmedabad and Departure
On your last day, visit the Akshardham Temple, Iskon Temple, Sabarmati Ashram, and Vaishnodevi Temple in Ahmedabad. In the evening, take a flight back to Chennai and bid farewell to Gujarat ReadMore
