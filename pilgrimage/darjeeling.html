Are you looking for an exciting getaway to the Northeast of India? Look no further than our Gangtok, Darjeeling, and Pelling trip! With 6 days and 5 nights of adventure, you'll experience breathtaking views, local culture, and delicious cuisine.


This package includes round trip air tickets from Bangalore to Bagdogra, private transfers and sightseeing, Natula Pass entry, 3-star standard accommodation with breakfast and dinner, and all currently applicable taxes, tolls, and parking. The package price for 6 pax is Rs. 1,68,900.


Please note that the package excludes everything except for the inclusions mentioned above.

Day 1: Arrival and Transfer to Gangtok

Upon arrival at New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) / Bagdogra Airport (IXB) / Tenzing Norgay Bus Stand (Junction), our representative will be there to assist with your transfer to Gangtok. Nestled in the hills of Sikkim, Gangtok is a beautiful town with a rich culture and history. Check into your 3-star accommodation and enjoy an evening free to explore M G Road (Mall) or the local shopping center.

Day 2: Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir (Full Day)

Start the day with breakfast at your hotel before embarking on an excursion to Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir. The lake is oval-shaped and nearly 50 feet deep, and is home to Brahmini ducks and many migratory birds. Nearby is the sacred Baba Mandir, known to be a very holy site for all. If you're feeling adventurous, you can opt for a tour of Nathu La Pass to the Republic of China. In the evening, enjoy some free time to explore M G Road (Mall) or the local shopping center.

Day 3: Pelling Transfer Via Namchi Sightseeing

After breakfast, drive to South Sikkim to visit Temi Tea Garden, Sapdhruptse, Sai Mandir, and Namchi CharDham. Then, transfer to Pelling, a beautiful settlement known for its botanical and zoological richness, as well as its historical sites, short trek routes, and wildlife. Check into your accommodation and enjoy an evening free to explore the small settlement.

Day 4: Pelling Local Sightseeing and Transfer to Darjeeling

After breakfast, embark on a sightseeing tour of Pelling. Visit Rimbi Water Falls, Sky Walk Rimbi Rock Garden, and Kechopalri Lake, one of the holiest Buddhist lakes. It is believed that any wish in front of the lake comes true. After your tour, transfer to Darjeeling, where you'll spend the night.

Day 5: Darjeeling Local Sightseeing

Rise early to drive to Tiger Hill to view the sunrise over Kanchendzonga Peak (subject to clear weather). On the way back, visit Ghoom Monastery, Peace Memorial, and Batasia Loop. After breakfast, enjoy a half-day city tour that includes Padmaja Naidu Zoological Park, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Ropeway, Tensing Gumpo Rock, Tibetan Refugee Self Help Center, Tea Estate, Natural History Museum, and Peace Pagoda. In the evening, enjoy some free time to stroll around the area.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Siliguri / New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP) / Bagdogra Airport (IXB) / Tenzing Norgay Bus Stand (Junction) for your return journey.

During your trip, you will stay at a variety of 3-star accommodations. In Gangtok, you'll stay at Hotel Woodberry & Spa or Hotel Golden Imperial & Spa. In Darjeeling, you'll stay at Hotel Dekeling Resort or Hotel Nirvana Resort. In Pelling, you'll stay at Hotel Olive & Spa or Hotel Chestnut Resort.
