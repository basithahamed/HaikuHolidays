ENQUIRY Welcome to Haiku Holidays, your trusted travel agency for exploring the rich cultural heritage of Madhya Pradesh through our Madhya Pradesh Jyothirlinga Trip.

Embark on a spiritual journey with us as we take you on a 3-night and 4 days trip to visit the famous Jyotirlinga temples in Ujain, Indore, Omkareshwar, and Mandu. Our package includes round-trip air tickets, 3-star accommodation, all meals, private AC transfers and sightseeing, toll, parking, driver beta, guide, and all taxes, so all you have to do is sit back and enjoy the trip.



With our package:

On Day 1 :
We'll catch a morning flight from Chennai to Indore, and proceed towards the holy city of Ujjain, where Lord Shiva himself resided. There, we'll visit the famous Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga temple, as well as the significant shrines of Bhartrihari Caves and Pir Matsyendranath temple. Finally, we'll pay a visit to the Sakthipeedth Maa temple and the Kala Bairavar temple. After a day of spiritual exploration, we'll retire to our hotel in Indore for a good night's rest.

On Day 2 :
We'll leave early in the morning to visit the Omkareshwar temple, situated on an island on the banks of the Narmada river. Here, we'll visit the Mamleshwar Jyotirlinga temple, as well as the significant spiritual centers of Siddhanath temple and Bhagvatpaada temple. After a day of exploration, we'll retire for the night in the beautiful town of Maheshwar, situated on the banks of the Narmada river.

On Day 3 :
We'll explore Maheshwar, known for its rich cultural heritage and beautiful temples. We'll visit the Maheshwar Shiva temple, one of the most prominent Shiva temples in India, as well as the beautiful palaces of Jahaj Mahal and Hindola Mahal. After a day of sightseeing, we'll catch a night flight back to Chennai, feeling spiritually rejuvenated and refreshed. ReadMore
