Are you tired of the hustle and bustle of city life? Do you long for a place where you can unwind, relax and recharge your batteries? Look no further than the Andaman Islands, a tropical paradise nestled in the Bay of Bengal. With Haiku Holidays' Andaman Trip package, you can experience the charm and beauty of this pristine destination in all its glory. Our 5-day trip takes you through the stunning islands, sandy beaches, and pristine blue waters, offering you an escape from the mundane routines of everyday life.


Our package includes everything you need for a comfortable and hassle-free trip. From round-trip airfare to 4-night accommodation, ferry transfers, delicious meals, sightseeing and more, we've got you covered. Our knowledgeable guides will ensure that you don't miss a thing as you explore the beauty of the Andaman Islands.


Here's a glimpse of what you can expect on our 5-day Andaman Trip:

Day 1

Upon your arrival in Port Blair, our team will be there to welcome you and transfer you to your hotel. After checking in, you'll be taken to the beautiful Corbin's Cove Beach, where you can soak up the sun and enjoy the tranquil surroundings. In the evening, we'll take you to the Cellular Jail to witness the captivating Sound and Light Show, which tells the story of the Indian freedom struggle. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 2

After a hearty breakfast, get ready to explore some of the best marine life and corals in the world. We'll take you to Ross Island, North Bay, and Coral Island, where you can see some of the most magnificent underwater creatures. You'll be awestruck by the vibrant colors and diversity of marine life that you'll see. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 3

After breakfast, we'll take you on a ferry to Havelock Island, where you can explore the famous Radhanagar Beach, one of the best beaches in Asia. With its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and scenic views, Radhanagar is a feast for the senses. Overnight stay in Havelock.

Day 4

After breakfast, you'll visit the beautiful Kalapathar Beach, known for its stunning black rocks and tranquil surroundings. Spend some time soaking up the sun and relaxing on the beach before we head back to Port Blair. Overnight stay in Port Blair.

Day 5

After breakfast, check out of your hotel and board your flight back to Chennai, refreshed and rejuvenated from your Andaman Trip.
