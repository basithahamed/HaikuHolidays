Are you looking for a perfect getaway to explore the rich cultural heritage of India? Then, Haiku Holidays has got you covered! Our 4 Nights/5 Days Delhi - Agra - Jaipur Trip package is the perfect opportunity to witness the magnificent historical monuments, royal palaces, and vibrant culture of North India


Starting from Chennai, this package includes roundtrip air tickets, private AC transfers and sightseeing, and luxurious 5-star accommodation with breakfast.

Day 1 : Arrival in Delhi

Board the early morning flight from Chennai International Airport to reach Delhi by 9:40 AM. After check-in to your 5-star accommodation, start your Delhi sightseeing tour. Visit popular landmarks such as the India Gate, Qutab Minar, and Lotus Temple, and indulge in some shopping. Spend the night at the hotel in Delhi.

Day 2 : Agra

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to visit the Akshardham Temple and Red Fort. Post-lunch, head towards Mathura and Vrindavan to seek blessings from Lord Krishna. Overnight stay in Agra.

Day 3 : Agra-Jaipur

Begin your day by visiting the iconic Taj Mahal and Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Post that, continue your journey to Jaipur and check in to your luxurious 5-star accommodation. Spend the evening at leisure.

Day 4 : Jaipur

After breakfast, start your Jaipur sightseeing tour with a visit to the magnificent Nahargarh Fort, followed by the stunning Amer Fort. Continue your tour with a visit to the Birla Mandir, Albert Museum Hall, and Jal Mahal. Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.

Day 5 : Departure

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and visit the Hawa Mahal and City Palace. Spend the afternoon exploring the local markets before departing for the airport in the evening. Board the flight to Chennai with unforgettable memories.
