Are you ready for a trip to paradise? Look no further than our Goa vacation package, a 3-night, 4-day adventure that will take you to some of the most stunning locations in India. With our affordable prices and unbeatable itinerary, you won't want to miss out on this opportunity.

Day 1 : Arrival in Goa
Upon arrival in Goa, you'll be greeted by our team and escorted to your luxurious 4-star hotel. After checking in, take some time to relax and unwind at the nearby beach. Enjoy the cool breeze and breathtaking views as you prepare for an exciting adventure ahead.

Day 2 : South Goa Exploration
After a hearty breakfast, it's time to embark on a journey through South Goa. Visit the stunning Tropical Spice plantation, the majestic Mahalaxmi Temple, and the awe-inspiring Old Goa church and museum. Take a stroll through the Panjim market and marvel at the stunning St. Cathedral church in Panaji. End your day with a relaxing sunset at Dona Paula and a thrilling boat cruise.

Day 3 : North Goa Sightseeing
On day 3, explore the beaches of North Goa, including Sinquerim, Calangute, Baga, Anjuna, and Vagator. Take in the stunning scenery and enjoy the warm sunshine as you relax on the sandy shores. After a full day of beach hopping, catch a night flight back to Chennai or Bangalore.

Day 4 : Shopping and Departure
Before departing, take some time for shopping and souvenir hunting. Bring back a piece of Goa with you as you board your night flight back home.

Our package includes round-trip airfare, 3 nights of luxurious 4-star accommodation with breakfast and dinner, private air-conditioned transfers, and a thrilling island trip that includes snorkeling, scuba diving, parasailing, and water biking. All taxes are also included, so you won't have to worry about any extra costs.
