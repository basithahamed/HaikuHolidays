Are you searching for a calm and beautiful escape? Look no further than our Kashmir trip package! This 5-night, 6-day trip will take you on a journey through some of the most breathtaking locations in the region. With everything from cable cars to wildlife zoos and more, this package is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike


Our package includes round-trip air tickets, private transfers, and sightseeing, 3- star standard accommodation with breakfast and dinner in Srinagar, a 1-night houseboat stay, and all currently applicable taxes. Please note that everything else is excluded from the package.

Day 1

Upon arriving at Srinagar airport, we'll take you to the stunning Dal Lake, which is known for its peaceful waters and beautiful surroundings. After your time at Dal Lake, we'll take you to the Tulip Garden, which is home to over a million tulips in various colors and is a sight to behold. Finally, we'll visit the Shankaracharya Temple, which is a sacred Hindu temple located on top of a hill and offers panoramic views of the valley. You will spend the night in a houseboat for a truly unique experience

Day 2

Start your day off with a delicious breakfast before heading to Sonmarg, which is often referred to as the "Meadow of Gold." Here, you'll see the majestic Thajwas Glacier, which is known for its breathtaking beauty. Next, we'll take you to Kangan Valley, which is famous for its stunning views and fresh air. Finally, we'll visit the Zojila Pass view, which is one of the highest passes in India and offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. Spend the night back in Srinagar.

Day 3

We'll take you on a trip to Gulmarg, which is known for its picturesque beauty and snowcapped mountains. Here, you'll visit Tangmarg, a beautiful town located in the foothills of the Himalayas. You'll also have the opportunity to go snow skiing, which is one of the most popular activities in the region. In addition, we'll take you to see the world-famous cable car, which is the highest cable car in the world and offers stunning views of the valley. Spend the night back in Srinagar.

Day 4

After breakfast, we'll head to Pahalgam, which is often referred to as the "Valley of Shepherds." Here, you'll see the beautiful Aru Valley, which is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular destination for nature lovers. You'll also visit the Betab Valley, which is famous for its lush greenery and crystal-clear water. Finally, we'll take you to the Himalayan Wildlife Zoo, where you can see a variety of animals that are native to the region. In addition, you can even try your hand at rafting and trout fishing in the Lidder River, which is a popular activity in the area. Spend the night in Pahalgam.

Day 5

We'll take you to the Kesar Garden and Apple Garden, which are both famous for their beautiful flowers and fruits. Here, you can enjoy a peaceful stroll and take in the beauty of the surroundings. Spend your final night in Srinagar.

Day 6

On this last day of the trip, we'll drop you off at Srinagar airport and you'll take a flight back to Chennai.

Don't miss out on this amazing opportunity to explore the wonders of Kashmir. Book now and get ready for an unforgettable journey!
