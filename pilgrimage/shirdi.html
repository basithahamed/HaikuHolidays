In Mumbai, you will visit:

Siddhivinayak Temple: This is a great place to visit! It's a popular Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha that's located in Prabhadevi, Mumbai. You'll love the peaceful ambiance and positive energy there, and the beautiful architecture and tranquil environment will transport you to a world of peace and serenity.

Mahalakshmi Temple: One of the most popular temples in Mumbai, dedicated to the Hindu goddess of wealth, fortune, and prosperity. The temple is located in the Mahalaxmi area and is known for its stunning architecture and beautiful surroundings.

Bandra-Worli Sea Link: A beautiful cable-stayed bridge that connects the suburbs of Bandra and Worli. The bridge offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea and Mumbai's skyline. This architectural masterpiece is a must-visit destination in Mumbai.

Gateway of India: A historic monument that was built during the British Raj in India. The monument overlooks the Arabian Sea and is a perfect spot for photography and sightseeing.

In Shirdi, you will visit:

Sai Baba Temple: One of the most revered spiritual destinations in India. The temple is known for its peaceful ambiance and positive energy. It is a place of worship for people of all religions and is believed to have the power to fulfill your wishes. We offer a VIP darshan at the temple for an unforgettable experience.

Kalaram Temple: A temple dedicated to Lord Rama and located in the old part of the town. The temple is known for its stunning architecture and is considered one of the most important landmarks in the area.

Sita Gufa Caves: A must-visit destination for nature lovers and those seeking peace and tranquility. The caves are located in a picturesque setting surrounded by lush greenery and serene natural beauty.
