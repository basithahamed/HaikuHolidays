Our Package Includes,
Sightseeing tour of Mussoorie, including Kempty Waterfalls, Gun Hill Point, and Mall Road
Jeep safari in Corbett National Park to spot tigers, elephants, and other wildlife
Visit to Naina Devi Temple, Hanuman Garhi, and Naina Peak in Nainital
3 Star Standard Accommodation with breakfast and dinner
Accommodation and transportation throughout the tour
Experienced tour guide to provide information and assistance

Escape to the beautiful hill stations of Mussoorie and Nainital with our 6-day MussorieNainital trip. This tour is great for newlyweds who want to spend some time enjoying the peaceful mountains and green landscapes.


Day 1

The tour starts from Delhi airport/station, where you will be picked up and driven to Mussoorie. Established by the British in 1823, Mussoorie offers breathtaking views of the Doon Valley and the distant Himalayas. Spend the evening at leisure and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 2

After a hearty breakfast, embark on a sightseeing tour of Mussoorie. Visit the famous Kempty Waterfalls, Gun Hill Point, and Mall Road, where you can indulge in some shopping. Spend the evening at leisure and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 3

Proceed to the jungles of Corbett after breakfast. You can choose to enjoy the evening safari at an additional price. Jim Corbett National Park is known for animals like tigers, elephants, and various species of birds. Spend the night in Corbett.

Day 4

Enjoy a jeep safari in the buffer zone on a sharing basis, where you may spot the Royal Bengal Tiger, Indian Hog Deer, and Asiatic Black Bear. Later, proceed to Nainital, where you have the rest of the day at leisure. Nainital is Kumaon's largest town and favorite hill resort situated around Naini Lake.

Day 5

After breakfast, visit Naina Devi Temple, Hanuman Garhi, and Naina Peak, where you can enjoy panoramic vistas of snow-capped Himalayas and Nainital Valley. In the evening, visit the local market at Mall Road, where you can shop for different types of candles.

Day 6

After breakfast, visit Naina Devi Temple, Hanuman Garhi, and Naina Peak, where you can enjoy panoramic vistas of snow-capped Himalayas and Nainital Valley. In the evening, visit the local market at Mall Road, where you can shop for different types of candles.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, it's time to bid farewell to this beautiful tour and transfer to Delhi Airport/Railway Station to board your flight/train to your next destination.
